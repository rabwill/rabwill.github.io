About me

I am a SharePoint/.Net developer/consultant currently working in Brisbane. I design and develop solutions that help people be more productive and engaged. In the O365 space, I believe in minimising customisation and maximising the utilisation of the products available to clients.

I am currently exploring the brave new world of SPFX, Microsoft Teams, Azure, FLOW and PowerApps. I am also a fan of modern sites in SharePoint (my bread and butter)

I like contributing thru github / blogs / twitter in my free time , it's my way of detoxing :)

When not coding I love to travel (read eat and drink, also an avid foodie) and play a good game of UNO with my 6-year old ;)
