##My Developer Essentials

###Code References
It’s a very fortunate time to be a developer with the community being so active and passionate about development.
It amazes me to see how much code people share online with the community and it’s free.
My most favourite sample are here- [sp-dev-fx-webparts](https://github.com/SharePoint/sp-dev-fx-webparts/tree/master/samples) (obviously cause I love SPFx !)
​
###Postman
For all my API requests while developing, I use [Postman](https://www.getpostman.com/). It saves a lot of time before actually developing the app and looking for answers.
To set up your development SharePoint tenancy (which is like a one time thing) I use this [post](http://www.ktskumar.com/2017/01/access-sharepoint-online-using-postman/), I find his language simple and easy to follow.
​
###JSON Viewer
Sometimes I would like to view my JSON data in a better way to work out the results and I use this online [jsonviewer](http://jsonviewer.stack.hu/).

###CSS Tricks
 Don't be fooled by the name of the website, they are not just designers!
What makes [CSS Tricks](https://css-tricks.com/) a one stop shop for all my design & development fulfilment as I call it, is the way their website looks.
Now these guys are serious about their development. Find great blogs for design, react tips and tricks here for your app.
